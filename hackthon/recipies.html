Avocado & Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 ripe avocado, diced
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, avocado, cucumber, onion, tomatoes, and cilantro.
  2. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the salad.
  3. Toss gently to combine and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve immediately or chill in the fridge for 30 minutes before serving.

Grilled Chicken & Veggie Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, grilled
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets, steamed
  • 1/2 cup roasted sweet potatoes
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with olive oil, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Grill the chicken over medium heat for 5-7 minutes per side or until cooked through.
  3. In a bowl, layer cooked brown rice, steamed broccoli, roasted sweet potatoes, and bell peppers.
  4. Slice the grilled chicken and place on top of the veggie bowl.
  5. Serve with a drizzle of olive oil and enjoy!
