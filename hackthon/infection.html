What to Eat When You Have a Fever

Foods to Eat:

  • Fluids: Water, clear broths, diluted fruit juices
  • Fruits: Oranges, strawberries, kiwi
  • Vegetables: Cooked spinach, carrots, potatoes
  • Lean Protein: Grilled chicken, fish, eggs
  • Whole Grains: Brown rice, oatmeal

Foods to Avoid:

  • Spicy foods
  • Fried foods
  • Sugary foods
  • Dairy products (optional, may worsen congestion)
  • Caffeine and alcohol

Tips:

  • Eat small, frequent meals.
  • Listen to your body.
  • Get plenty of rest.
