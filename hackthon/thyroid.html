What to Eat When You Have a Thyroid

Foods to Eat:

  • Iodine-Rich Foods: Iodized salt, seaweed, fish, dairy, eggs
  • Selenium-Rich Foods: Brazil nuts, sunflower seeds, fish, lean meats
  • Zinc-Rich Foods: Shellfish, legumes, nuts, seeds
  • Fruits & Vegetables: Berries, leafy greens, sweet potatoes
  • Healthy Fats: Avocado, olive oil, fatty fish

Foods to Avoid:

  • Goitrogens: Raw cruciferous vegetables, soy products
  • Processed Foods: Fast food, packaged snacks
  • Excessive Caffeine & Alcohol: Coffee, energy drinks, alcohol
  • Gluten: Wheat, barley, rye (for autoimmune thyroid disorders)
  • High-Sugar Foods: Sweets, sugary cereals
