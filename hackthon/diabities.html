What to Eat When You Have a Diabetes

Foods to Eat:

  • Whole Grains: Brown rice, quinoa, oats, barley
  • Non-Starchy Vegetables: Broccoli, spinach, kale
  • Lean Proteins: Chicken, turkey, fish, tofu, legumes
  • Healthy Fats: Avocado, nuts, seeds, olive oil
  • Low-Glycemic Fruits: Berries, apples, oranges

Foods to Avoid:

  • Sugary Foods: Sweets, pastries, sugary drinks
  • Refined Carbohydrates: White bread, white rice
  • Trans Fats: Fried foods, packaged baked goods
  • High-Glycemic Fruits: Watermelon, pineapple
  • Caffeinated & Sugary Drinks: Soda, sweetened coffee

Tips:

  • Eat small, balanced meals at regular intervals.
  • Choose foods with a low glycemic index.
  • Stay hydrated with water or unsweetened beverages.
  • Track your carbohydrate intake carefully.
  • Consult a dietitian for personalized advice.
