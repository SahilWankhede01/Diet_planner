What to Eat When You Have a Blood pressure

Foods to Eat:

  • Fruits: Bananas, oranges, berries, melons
  • Vegetables: Spinach, broccoli, carrots, sweet potatoes
  • Whole Grains: Brown rice, oatmeal, quinoa
  • Lean Protein: Skinless poultry, fish, legumes
  • Low-Fat Dairy: Skim milk, low-fat yogurt
  • Healthy Fats: Avocados, olive oil, nuts

Foods to Avoid:

  • Salty Foods: Chips, canned soups, processed snacks
  • Fried Foods: French fries, fried chicken
  • High-Sugar Foods: Sweets, sugary drinks
  • Processed Meats: Bacon, sausages, deli meats
  • Excessive Alcohol: Limit to moderate levels

Tips:

  • Limit sodium intake to less than 2,300 mg per day.
  • Stay hydrated with plenty of water.
  • Incorporate regular physical activity.
  • Monitor blood pressure regularly.
  • Consult a doctor for personalized advice.
